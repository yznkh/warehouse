Entity's SOC and SIEM Enablement Questionnaire (SOC)

Purpose and Instructions

As part of our national effort to strengthen cybersecurity across critical sectors, this questionnaire aims to assess the current cybersecurity monitoring capabilities of individual entities and support the establishment of Entity's SOC Capability to integrate with the Sectorial CERT.

Confidentiality disclaimer

All information provided through this questionnaire will be treated with the strictest confidentiality and used solely for the purpose of enhancing national cybersecurity. The data is stored in a secure environment with the highest standards of protection in place.

This questionnaire is divided into two essential parts:

Part 1 : SOC Maturity and Readiness Assessment

This section is designed to help us evaluate your entity’s current Security Operations Center (SOC) maturity and its readiness to connect to the Sectorial CERT. The information collected will allow us to:

  • Understand your existing cybersecurity monitoring infrastructure and practices across IT and OT/IoT/IIoT environments.
  • Assess the level of integration, governance, compliance, and incident response capabilities.
  • Identify gaps and opportunities for capacity building, advanced services, and integration with sectorial cybersecurity infrastructure.

Part 2: Asset Inventory and Technical Sizing Inputs

This section focuses on gathering key technical and operational inputs required for:

  • Understanding the existing SOC Monitoring capabilities for entities that have SOC/SIEM capabilities.
  • Sizing the SOC and SIEM infrastructure needed to be implemented at entities who don't have SOC/SIEM Monitoring Capabilities.
  • Providing realistic data to potential bidders to enable accurate design, scoping, and cost estimation for SIEM deployment, log ingestion, threat detection, and SOC services.
  • Understanding the scale, diversity, and criticality of your IT and OT assets, including network devices, servers, endpoints, ICS/SCADA components, IoT/IIoT/IoMT systems, and communication protocols.

Instructions for Completing the Questionnaire

  • Please answer all questions to the best of your knowledge. Where estimations are necessary, indicate accordingly.
  • For multiple-choice or checklist-style questions, select all options that apply.
  • If additional context or clarification is needed, please use the optional comment fields.
  • Your input is essential to ensure the successful planning, deployment, and integration of a Entity's SOC and the Sectorial CERT
